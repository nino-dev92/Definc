About The CEO

I am Ifeoluwa Osunkeye, a law graduate from Babcock university but has always had a nack for the show business hence my current line of profession. From my educational background I am strict and disciplined when it comes to work ethics. I went to further this dream by doing a course in Television and radio practice from the E Academy Ghana, entered for a Radio reality show in Ghana and was second runner up at the end of the competition. This launched my radio career as “midnight cruise” Co anchor on YFm Ghana till i was able to bag my own show on an online radio station. (Afreetune.com) After a few years of working experience in Ghana I decided to bring it back home where I am currently a TV presenter, red carpet host as well as a Tv Producer for @plustvafrica. I am also a contributing member to the content creation team. I pride myself in story telling and bringing to life societal issues in details yet entertaining. I am also a voice over artist. I am very industrious, ready to learn and get better, I am also a team player. About my personal life, I am probably the most friendly and goofy person on the surface of the earth. I live life by one philosophy “one day at a time”. Even though some call me the life of the party, I call myself an Introverted Extrovert. I put in in my all to whatever I set my mind to.
